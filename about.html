About Us



Puppy Tailrd is the hobby and ingenuity of Connie brought to life. Our founder takes measurements and creates whimsical dog sheaths for whomever so desires them.

In 2017 Connie's family adopted two sweet, short haired dogs from the south. These goofballs didn't know what hit them when they moved into their new abode in Minnesota. Connie in her benevolent grace took pity on these adorable hunyuks and made them some hoodies. These dogs love their sweaters so much that they pout when you take them off.

Connie's love for fiber construction and the dogs love for their new clothes inspired her. In January of 2019 she decided to take first steps toward making her passion into a business, thus Puppy Tailrd is born.
